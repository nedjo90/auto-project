Functional Specification – Auto & Motorcycle Classifieds Platform
1. Project Objective

Build a car and motorcycle classifieds platform focused on transparency, trust, and mandatory disclosure, with the goal of reducing hidden defects and overpriced listings.

2. Scope

Cars

Motorcycles

Listings published by:

Private sellers

Professional sellers

3. Listing Creation

A listing cannot be published unless all required information is provided.

General Information

Brand / Manufacturer

Model

Year of manufacture

Mileage

Fuel type

Transmission

Asking price

Location

Mandatory Transparency Requirements

Declared technical inspection (date + result)

Upload of the official technical inspection report

Signed declaration of honor stating that no known hidden defects exist

4. Market Price Comparison

The platform automatically compares the asking price with an estimated market price.

A visual indicator is displayed:

Below market price

Market-aligned

Above market price

5. CarVertical Report

A CarVertical report is mandatory for every listing.

Integrated cost: €4

Report displayed directly within the listing

Included data:

Mileage history

Accident records

Theft history

Country of origin

Ownership history

6. Buyer Experience

Advanced filters (budget, valid technical inspection, below market price, etc.)

Trust score based on declared data and third-party reports

7. Business Model

Fixed cost per listing: €15

Breakdown:

€4: CarVertical report

€11: Listing publication, automated checks, and moderation

No free listings are allowed.

8. Moderation

Automatic rejection if the technical inspection is missing

User reporting system

Suspension of fraudulent sellers

9. Future Enhancements

Identity verification

Seller ratings and reviews

Integrated insurance and financing options
